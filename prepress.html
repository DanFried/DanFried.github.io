Prepress.

Alright, once you have exposed, rinsed out and dried your screen it's time to prep it. Take it to a well lit area. Tape up the gap inside the frame between the frame a emulsion (pictured below). Following that check for pinholes, to be blocked out, or debris in the stencil. All good? Let's setup!!
